Welcome to La Curiosité...

We are incredibly excited to introduce you to La Curiosité, a newly launched online magazine run by students in the Department of French at the University of Bristol. You can expect to read a range of hot topics from travel, cuisine, year abroad experiences, studying advice, cultural activities and more! The content of the magazine is largely in English with some French. Our dedicated team is always looking for writers and content suggestions, so if you would like to get involved we would love to hear from you. We are excited to share this exciting opportunity with you and hope you will enjoy reading La Curiosité.
